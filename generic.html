This is a little about my journey

Hello! I'm Serenity! I am a compassionate and detail-oriented individual who has transitioned from a background in nursing to a career in software engineering.

My journey in healthcare has equipped me with valuable skills in problem-solving, critical thinking, and effective communication. As a nurse, I learned to navigate high-pressure situations, collaborate with diverse teams, and prioritize patient well-being. These experiences have seamlessly translated into my approach to software engineering.

Driven by a passion for technology and a desire to contribute to positive change, I've transitioned to software engineering to explore new ways of solving complex problems. I am currently proficient in HTML CSS, React, Vanilla JavaScript, and I am eager to continue expanding my technical skill set.

My commitment to enhancing patient care has now evolved into a commitment to creating efficient and impactful software solutions. I am excited about the intersection of healthcare and technology, and I am keen on leveraging my background to contribute to innovative projects in the software engineering space.

When I'm not immersed in code, you can find me advocating for the integration of technology in healthcare, exploring the latest advancements in software engineering, and connecting with fellow professionals who share a passion for innovation.

Let's connect and explore the endless possibilities that software engineering has to offer. Feel free to browse through my portfolio to see some of the exciting projects I'm working on!
